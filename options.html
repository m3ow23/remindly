Seeking a gentler browser productivity tool? Remindly is a simple website usage reminder for a better productivity.

Most website usage tools forcibly close your tabs when the timer runs out. Remindly on the other hand gently reminds you of your website usage depending on the remind interval you've set.

The extension allows you to continue using your defined apps whenever you truly need them and reminds you when not. This extension is designed for people who have enough self-discipline to control their usage when reminded.

Alternatively, if you lack self-discipline, stricter website usage management extensions may be more suitable. A personally tested and effective extension, is LeechBlock NG by James Anderson.
